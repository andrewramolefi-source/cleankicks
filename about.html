Founded in 2021, Clean Kicks is more than a sneaker laundry. We are a lifestyle brand built on empowerment, integrity, and community impact.

From a Garage to a Trusted Brand

What started in a small garage in Lawley Extension 1 has grown into a premier professional sneaker laundry service south of Johannesburg. Clean Kicks was established to address a critical market gap: the lack of professional shoe care in township and surrounding communities.

After receiving hands-on training from South Africa's first-ever sneaker laundry, we launched with a commitment to innovation and quality. Today, we clean over 100 pairs per month and employ a dedicated team of four professionals.

Community Initiative

Restoring Dignity Through Every Step

Clean Kicks believes that business success is incomplete without community impact. Our School Shoe Drive is a cornerstone of our social responsibility strategy, specifically designed to support students in underprivileged communities.

The Purpose

To bridge the gap for children who lack proper school footwear, ensuring they attend school with confidence and a sense of belonging.

Professional Restoration

We don't just donate; we restore. Every pair donated undergoes a professional deep wash and leather conditioning to ensure it is in excellent condition.

Our Process

01

02
Deep Chemical Cleanse

03
Distribution

The restored footwear is donated directly to students in need at local schools south of Johannesburg.

"A small gesture can lead to a giant leap for a child's future."

School Shoe Drive Initiative

Our School Shoe Drive is a natural extension of our mission to restore confidence from the ground up. We believe that a clean, decent pair of shoes can change how a learner walks into a classroom with pride, dignity, and self-belief. Through this initiative, we collect new and gently used school shoes, carefully refurbish them to the highest standard, and donate them to children in need. It’s more than just footwear; it’s about giving young learners the confidence to focus, participate, and perform at their best because when a child feels confident, they show up differently for their future.
